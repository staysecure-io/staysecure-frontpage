Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how StaySecure uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our service.

What data and where do we collect

You directly provide StaySecure your personal profile information (name, e-mail address, optional phone number) when you register account or update account data. We also collect and process data when you use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.

How will we use your data

StaySecure collects your data so that we can manage your account and send you notifications.

We will never share or sold your data to anyone.

How do we store your data

All data in StaySecure is stored securely with at rest encryption. We send data only over HTTPS transport layer security (TLS) encrypted connections for additional security. Our data center is located in Falkenstein, Germany.

We will keep your data for the time your account exists. After that, your data will be removed.

Your data protection rights

StaySecure would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access. You have the right to request StaySecure for copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification. You have the right to request that StaySecure correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request StaySecure to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure. You have the right to request that StaySecure erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing. You have the right to request that StaySecure restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing. You have the right to object to StaySecure processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability. You have the right to request that StaySecure transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: info@staysecure.io

Cookies and how we use them

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

StaySecure uses cookies in a range of ways, including: keeping you signed in and understanding how you use our website. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used. You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and remove cookies from your browser. However, our website may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

Our website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

StaySecure keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on the 21 September 2019.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about StaySecure privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us via e-mail: info@staysecure.io

Should you wish to report a complaint or if you feel that Our Company has not addressed your concern in a satisfactory manner, you may contact the Information Commissioner’s Office.
